Introduction

This guide explains the complete method, performance flow, and hidden features for the ATMA web application. Please review this document carefully to ensure a smooth and effective performance.

The Input Method

ATMA uses a gesture-based system to covertly register the spectator's choices. The method relies on the STARTING AREA of a single SWIPE LEFT gesture for all five options.

The screen is divided into five invisible zones. Where you begin your swipe determines the selection. The grid below provides a visual representation of these zones and their corresponding choices.

  • To select Choice 1: Start your SWIPE LEFT from the Top-Left quadrant.
  • To select Choice 2: Start your SWIPE LEFT from the Top-Right quadrant.
  • To select Choice 3: Start your SWIPE LEFT from the Bottom-Right quadrant.
  • To select Choice 4: Start your SWIPE LEFT from the Bottom-Left quadrant.
  • To select Choice 5: Start your SWIPE LEFT from the Center area of the screen.

Note: A single, decisive swipe left is all that is required. After a valid gesture is registered, the app will automatically advance to the next screen.

Performance Flow

  1. Introduce the Premise: Explain the nature of the experiment. On your phone, you will present three separate lists to the spectator, each containing five choices. The app will start on the first list screen (Victim).
  2. First Choice (Victim): Present the list of names. When the spectator chooses, perform a single SWIPE LEFT starting from the zone corresponding to their choice. This action registers their selection and automatically slides to the next list (Location).
  3. Second Choice (Location): The spectator chooses a location from the new list. Again, perform a single SWIPE LEFT from the correct starting zone. The app advances to the final list (Weapon).
  4. Third Choice (Weapon): The spectator chooses a weapon. Perform the final SWIPE LEFT from the appropriate zone.
  5. QR Code Reveal: After the third input, the app displays the final QR code. Instruct the spectator to scan it with their phone.
  6. The Revelation: The spectator's phone will open the news article. Allow them to read and discover that the details match their choices, then guide their attention to the photograph for the final effect.

System Controls & Features

  • Language Selection: To open the settings menu and switch the article's language, perform a two-finger swipe down on any of the list screens.
  • Reset App: To reset the app for a new performance, perform a quick triple-tap on the final QR code screen. The app will return to the first list.
  • View Input History: To confirm your inputs, press and hold your finger on the QR code screen. An overlay showing your registered choices will appear and will vanish when you lift your finger.

Operational Tips

  • Lock Screen Rotation: Before performing, ensure your phone's screen rotation is locked. This prevents the layout from shifting unexpectedly if the device is tilted.
  • Natural Handling: Hold the phone naturally in one hand. Using the thumb of the same hand to perform the swipe gestures often looks the most casual and disarming.
  • Practice: Rehearse the gesture sequences until they become automatic. Your actions should appear casual and natural.
